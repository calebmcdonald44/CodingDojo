The Fighter

Tommy Gunn's career is hard to rank. We only see the beginning of it, and mostly through montages where he's knocking out lesser fighters. What we do know is this- he never went the distance in a fight, and he lost to a brain-damaged, out-of-shape Rocky who was actively suffering from PTSD flashbacks during the fight. Considering that and his easily exploitable temper, you can't really justify putting him any higher.
